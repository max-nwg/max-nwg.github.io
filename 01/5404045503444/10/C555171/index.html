Care Instructions Exclusive Of Decoration
With the following tips for caring for your clothes, you can extend their life and minimize the environmental impact of the care process.
We consciously choose a timeless, neutral design. By taking into account the needs of the wearer and eco-design, in combination with quality materials, you can enjoy optimal longevity.

- Wash efficiently: Use a full load and select a low-spin setting. Washing at lower temperatures conserves energy and is gentler on fabrics, preserving their quality.
- Protect the fabric: Turn clothes inside out before washing. This shields the outer surface, reducing wear and maintaining their appearance.
- Air dry for sustainability: Skip the dryer to save energy and minimize fabric damage. Air drying is a gentle, eco-friendly alternative that extends the life of your garments.

By adopting these practices, you can keep your clothes looking their best while supporting a more sustainable future.
